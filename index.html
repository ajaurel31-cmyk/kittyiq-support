About KittyIQ

KittyIQ helps you track your cat's health, decode their behavior, and learn training techniques — all in one app.

  • Health tracking (weight, feeding, medications, vet visits)
  • Behavior pattern decoder with photo analysis
  • Step-by-step training lessons
  • Health insights and trend analysis
  • PDF health reports for vet visits

Frequently Asked Questions

Is my data private?

Yes. All your data is stored locally on your device. KittyIQ does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information with third parties.

What's included in the free version?

Free users get 1 cat profile, basic health tracking, 4 training lessons, and 3 behavior scans per day.

What does Premium include?

Premium unlocks unlimited cat profiles, all training lessons, unlimited behavior scans, advanced health insights, and PDF health report export — all for a one-time purchase of $4.99. No subscriptions, no recurring charges. Pay once and it's yours forever.

Is Premium a subscription?

No. KittyIQ Premium is a one-time purchase of $4.99. There are no recurring charges or hidden fees. You buy it once and keep it forever, including any future updates to premium features.

How do I restore my purchase on a new device?

Open KittyIQ, go to Profile > KittyIQ Premium, and tap "Restore Purchases." Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID used for the original purchase.

Is this a substitute for veterinary care?

No. KittyIQ is for informational and educational purposes only. Always consult a licensed veterinarian for your cat's health concerns.
