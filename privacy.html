KittyIQ Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 8, 2026

Overview

KittyIQ ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Data You Provide:

  • Cat profile information (name, breed, age, weight, photos)
  • Health records (feeding, weight, medications, vet visits)
  • Training progress and preferences
  • App settings and preferences

Automatically Collected:

  • Basic device information for app functionality
  • App usage analytics (anonymized)
  • Crash reports for improving stability

Data Storage

All your cat data, health records, and photos are stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. Your data never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to export or share it. We do not have access to your personal data.

Camera & Photo Library

KittyIQ requests access to your camera and photo library solely for the purpose of adding photos to cat profiles and using the Behavior Decoder feature. Photos are processed on-device and are not uploaded to any server.

Behavior Analysis

The Behavior Decoder provides educational insights about cat body language based on common feline behavioral patterns. Photos you take or select are stored locally on your device and are not uploaded to any server. All analysis is performed on-device.

Notifications

If you enable notifications, KittyIQ uses Apple's local notification system to send reminders for medications, feeding schedules, and health check-ups. These notifications are generated on your device and do not involve any external service.

Subscriptions

Premium subscriptions are processed entirely through Apple's App Store. We do not collect or store any payment information. Subscription management is handled by Apple per their terms and conditions.

Third-Party Services

KittyIQ does not share your data with third-party services. We do not use third-party analytics, advertising networks, or data brokers.

Children's Privacy

KittyIQ is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access all your data (stored on your device)
  • Export your data as PDF reports
  • Delete your data by removing the app
  • Opt out of notifications at any time

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, you can reach us through the App Store listing or leave a review with your feedback.
